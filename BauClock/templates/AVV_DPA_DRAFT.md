# BauClock — AVV / DPA Draft

## Status

Draft data processing agreement template.
For controller/processor relationship between client company and BauClock provider.
This is not final legal advice.

---

## 1. Parties and roles

Customer = controller
Provider = processor

---

## 2. Subject matter

Processing of personal data in connection with provision of BauClock SaaS services, including working time tracking, role-based access, site attendance support and related support/export functions.

---

## 3. Duration

For the duration of the underlying service relationship and any agreed post-termination handling period.

---

## 4. Nature and purpose of processing

Purposes may include:
- user and role administration
- company/site assignment
- time event recording
- summary calculation
- payment-support workflow handling
- subcontractor coordination
- export/support output generation
- security, logging and support operations

---

## 5. Categories of data subjects

May include:
- company users
- workers/employees
- subcontractor company representatives
- accountants / managers / site managers

---

## 6. Categories of personal data

May include:
- identity and contact data
- role and company assignment data
- time-event data
- site assignment data
- limited geodata where used for site presence verification
- payment-support related data
- audit/security logs

---

## 7. Processor obligations

Processor shall:
- process only on documented instructions
- ensure confidentiality
- implement appropriate TOMs
- support controller with data subject rights where applicable
- support security incident handling
- delete/return data according to agreement and policy
- maintain subprocessor transparency

---

## 8. Controller obligations

Controller remains responsible for:
- lawfulness of workforce data processing
- proper internal information to workers
- correct use of the service
- role and access assignments on customer side
- review of outputs before external legal/accounting reliance

---

## 9. Technical and organizational measures

TOMs should be attached or linked and include at minimum:
- access control
- tenant isolation
- transport/storage protection
- auditability
- backup/recovery
- incident handling
- retention/deletion procedures

---

## 10. Subprocessors

Provider shall maintain a subprocessor list and a defined change notification mechanism.

---

## 11. Data subject rights and support

Processor shall support controller with reasonable requests regarding:
- access
- rectification
- deletion
- restriction
- data portability where applicable
- objection handling where relevant

---

## 12. Incident handling

Processor shall notify controller of personal data breaches as required by law and contract.

---

## 13. End of processing

Upon end of services, return/deletion/retention handling follows the contract, retention policy and legal obligations.

---

## 14. Finalization note

This draft must be reviewed and finalized for real contractual use.
