# BauClock — Betriebsrat Rollout Note Draft

## Status

Draft note for client companies that have a Betriebsrat or other internal co-determination process.
This is a product template, not final legal advice.

---

## 1. Purpose

This note exists because introduction of an electronic working-time / attendance system may require internal coordination with employee representation depending on the client’s structure and legal obligations.

---

## 2. What the client should review internally

The client should review internally:
- whether a Betriebsrat exists
- whether rollout requires internal co-determination steps
- whether a Betriebsvereinbarung or similar internal agreement is needed
- who signs off on the rollout internally

---

## 3. What BauClock should provide to support rollout

The product/provider should be able to provide:
- system description
- privacy / processing summary
- role and access summary
- GPS/site-presence summary if applicable
- retention and auditability summary
- security/TOMs summary

---

## 4. Product handling

During company onboarding, the system should allow the client to indicate:
- Betriebsrat exists: yes/no
- internal rollout documents required: yes/no
- rollout completed: yes/no or pending

---

## 5. Finalization note

This template must be reviewed and finalized before use with real clients.
