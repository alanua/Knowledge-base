# Reusable donor kernels v1

## Scope
Short canonical extraction from donor audit for current Lavalamp effects work.

Hard lock:
- ESP32 / future ESP32-S3
- WLED base
- 16x16 closed cylindrical matrix
- matte diffuser
- cheap-render first

## Adopt now

### K1. Smooth Flow Substrate
Source:
- FireNoise / Noise2D

Use:
- continuous internal field motion
- no row-jumps
- no stepping
- base motion layer for Flame / Anemone / Jellyfish

Keep:
- temporal smoothness
- cheap full-field evolution

Reject:
- flat full-panel visual identity

### K2. Tentacle Bend Kernel
Source:
- Octopus

Use:
- soft bend from anchor to tip
- phase lag along tentacle length
- per-tentacle phase offsets

Keep:
- organic deformation
- few active tentacles only

Reject:
- literal octopus look
- too many arms

## Adopt partially

### K3. Macro Calm Evolution
Source:
- Sun Radiation

Use:
- slow global motion continuity
- calm scene-wide temporal drift

Keep:
- smooth macro timing

Reject:
- sun-ray visual identity

### K4. Subtle Inner Life
Source:
- Plasma Ball

Use:
- very weak internal living structure
- only as a secondary modifier

Keep:
- slight inner-channel feel

Reject:
- electric look
- aggressive sparkle
- dominant plasma behavior

## Per-scene mapping

### Flame
- K1 primary
- K3 secondary
- K4 only if very reduced

### Anemone
- K2 primary
- K1 secondary
- K3 secondary

### Jellyfish
- K2 primary for tentacles
- K1 primary for soft body field
- K3 secondary for pulse continuity
- K4 very weak only

## Reject for now
- donor cloning
- flat panel carry-over
- heavy pseudo-physics
- expensive multipass chains
- donor palettes as final identity
