# Finance Model

## Principles
- system is not full accounting software
- finance layer is optional for subcontractors and Gewerbe
- must support minimal and extended flows

## Minimal flow
- track hours
- export or share data with accountant

## Extended flow
- generate invoice
- track sent date
- track payment status
- manual payment confirmation

## Key rules
- accountant does not change contract conditions by default
- payments must be auditable
- manual corrections must be traceable

## Future
- DATEV export
- automated reporting
- multi-currency support (handled externally for now)
